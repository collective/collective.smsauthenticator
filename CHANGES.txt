0.2.9 (2014-10-03)
------------------------------------------------

  which doesn't need the twofactor properties. We shortcut the subscriber if

  http://docs.plone.org/external/plone.api/docs/api/exceptions.html#plone.api.exc.CannotGetPortalError
  [puittenbroek]
